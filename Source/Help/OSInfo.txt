OSInfo

Syntax: #osinfo

Gets the information of the current operating system.

Example:

#Operating System Information
#----------------------------
#Name = Microsoft Windows 10 Pro
#BuildVersion = 10240
#Edition =
#Service Pack = None
#Version = 10.0.10240.0
#ProcessorBits = Bit64
#OSBits = Bit64
#ProgramBits = Bit64
